The Process

More than an agency. I deliver agency-quality websites

As a developer of 8 years, there are three main steps necessary for the perfect project scope - discovery, design and development, and launching. Not only am I focusing on the design aspect, but the performance which is why I like to always improve for all my clients.

Discovery

During the discovery process, research is done on your business industry and competitors. The goals of the discovery phase are to outline the projet audience, purpose, structure, and goals.

Design and Development

Once the design is chosen, the development starts! Every website is custom developed without using any themes. The development process involves debugging and testing special features on the website as well as SEO.

Launch

Once you think the site is amazing, it'll be launched on your domain name and it will be constantly monitored to ensure optimal performance and experience!
